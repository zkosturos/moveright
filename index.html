The Star

We’re constantly being told what to see, what to believe, what to expect, and what’s possible.

And the degree to which we buy in is the degree to which we limit what's possible.

Everyone who has done something others thought couldn't be done has one thing in common.

They see differently.

They see faith when others see fear. They see opportunity when others see too much risk.

When you see differently, you believe differently. And when you believe differently, you move differently.

That’s what Move Right. is about.

If that sounds exciting to you, you're in the right place.

ABOUT ME

I'm Zach. Husband, father, and builder of a few things.

I run a commercial real estate firm, a construction company, and an investment firm. I deploy capital across real estate, equities, and small business acquisitions. Most days, my actual job is figuring out where to allocate money and attention.

Over the years, I’ve realized that nearly every meaningful outcome starts the same way:
